    MPPT Solar Inverter EVO Series 2000VA 3200VAS

    SCI-EVO-2000&3200 is the newest MPPT inverter in 2023, with built-in 80A MPPT controller, supporting PV input voltage from 30V-400VDC, only few solar panels are needed to operate the machine (35VDC open circuit voltage for the first start up, keep stable 30VDC input afterwards), reducing the cost of using the system.     General Features

    The MPPT Solar Inverter EVO Series, available in 2000VA and 3200VA models, stands out for its impressive features and versatility. These inverters are equipped with a built-in 80A MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) controller, which optimizes solar energy conversion to enhance system efficiency.

    One of the standout features is their wide PV input voltage range, supporting from 30V to 400VDC. This flexibility allows for a variety of solar panel configurations. For initial startup, the inverter requires a 35VDC open circuit voltage, but it stabilizes at a 30VDC input, making it adaptable to different solar setups.

    Cost efficiency is a key advantage of the EVO Series. The inverters are designed to operate effectively with only a few solar panels, which helps in reducing the overall cost of the solar power system. Additionally, the EVO Series is suitable for locations without mains supply or with unstable mains conditions, providing reliable power in challenging environments.
