    Tuncmatik UPS

    Tuncmatik Online UPSs are ideal for sensitive devices, as they isolate them from the network, charge batteries with network electricity, and filter network situations, providing clean energy to connected devices.



    Full wave sine wave output

    Full compatibility with all types of electrical devices, the ideal solution for your medical and similar critical applications.

    Wide input voltage range (110-300 VAC)

    Ability to work online even at very low and very high voltages without switching to battery.

    Input power factor correction (PF=0.99)

    It does not impose additional compensation load on your network. It saves money on your electricity bills.

    High output power factor (PF=0.9)

    It provides 28% more power at its output compared to standard 0.7 power factor UPSs. Supports more electrical/electronic devices.

    Smart charging technology for optimal battery performance

    Since it charges the battery with a special charging technique, it extends battery life, reduces your operating costs and saves money.

    High performance microprocessor

    Thanks to its digital structure and high speed, the CPU-controlled control card provides full protection by performing the functions of the UPS such as overload, short circuit, low-high voltage and overheat in a timely manner, thus ensuring that the UPS has a stable and reliable structure.

    Everything is under control with the multifunctional, easy-to-read LCD screen. Thanks to the newly developed LCD screen, functions, input-output values ​​and alarms can be easily monitored.
